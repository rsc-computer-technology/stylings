Instructional Services Manager

The main duties of the Instructional Services Manager for the Computer Technology Department are:

  1. Manage course development and ensure course alignment with industry certifications when applicable.
  2. Manage credit for prior learning opportunities for students.
  3. Assist in grant work for the department.
  4. Assist in resolving student and adjunct issues.
  5. Oversee department projects as needed.
  6. Manage the computer labs.

The Instructional Services Manager is Gordon Inman.

Email: Gordon.Inman@riosalado.edu

Phone: (480) 517-8307

Instructional Services Supervisor

The main duties of the Instructional Services Supervisor for the Computer Technology Department are:

  1. Work with adjuncts to ensure department policies and procedures are being followed.
  2. Verify all suggested course revisions, implement them, and notify all affected faculty members about changes.
  3. Work with the Instructional Helpdesk in training new Adjunct Faculty members.
  4. Coordinate the Dual Enrollment program for the department.
  5. Assist in resolving issues with students or within the department.
  6. Assist with department projects.
